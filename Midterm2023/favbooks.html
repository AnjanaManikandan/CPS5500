Anjana's Favorite Books


Percy Jackson

Percy Jackson is a series of fantasy novels by Rick Riordan. The series follows the adventures of Percy Jackson, a demigod and the son of Poseidon.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of fantasy novels by J.K. Rowling. The series follows the life and adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter.

Mortal Instruments

The Mortal Instruments is a series of fantasy novels by Cassandra Clare. A young girl lives in a world of demons and shadowhunters.
